\subsection{Contribution Ratio}
The project workload was shared equitably, with each member taking ownership of roughly equal portions of the complex logic and system verification. The contribution ratio is approximately:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Tam Siu Ho: 33\%
    \item YANG Shi Long: 33\%
    \item Yam Chun Ho: 33\%
    \item All members contributed equally to documentation and report writing.
\end{itemize}
